Pangea Intact

Central Connecticut is situated near the center of the supercontinent Pangea. The region is characterized by ancient, highly metamorphosed basement rocks from earlier mountain-building events (like the Appalachians).

Middletown Connection

The metamorphic rocks beneath Middletown today (like the Bolton Schist) were formed deep underground long before the dinosaurs.
