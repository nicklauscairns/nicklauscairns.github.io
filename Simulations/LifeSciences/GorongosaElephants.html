The Tuskless Elephants of Gorongosa

HS-LS4-4: Natural Selection & Adaptation

During the Mozambican Civil War (1977-1992), heavy poaching selectively targeted African elephants with tusks to finance the conflict. In this simulation, investigate how extreme human-induced selective pressure affects the genetic frequency of a naturally occurring, but normally rare, "tuskless" trait in female elephants over several generations.
