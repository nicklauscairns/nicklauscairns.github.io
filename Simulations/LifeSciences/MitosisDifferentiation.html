Scientific Context

Key NGSS Concepts:

  • Mitosis: Individual cells grow and divide, passing identical genetic material (DNA) to both daughter cells.
  • Differentiation: Cells specialize to form tissues and organs that work together to meet the needs of the whole organism.
  • Complexity: The organism begins as a single cell and produces a complex system through these two processes.
