Energy Transfer

Energy is required to break the bonds of Glucose and Oxygen. However, more energy is released when the new bonds of Carbon Dioxide and Water are formed.

This "net release" of energy is captured to build ATP (which transports energy to muscles) and released as heat (maintaining body temperature).
