Habitat Fragmentation & Genetics Mitigation

NGSS HS-LS4-6: Create or revise a simulation to test a solution to mitigate adverse impacts of human activity on biodiversity and genetic variation.

The Problem

A major highway has bisected the habitat of an endangered mountain lion population. The population is now split into "West" and "East" subpopulations.

Impact: Without genetic flow (mating across the highway), inbreeding depression occurs. Genetic diversity drops, increasing mortality rates and leading toward localized extinction.

Engineering Solutions

Select a solution to mitigate the impact. Constraints include the municipal budget ($2.0M max).
