Phenomenon & Task

Wind turbines convert the kinetic energy of wind into electrical energy. However, extracting maximum energy depends on the design. How can we optimize blade length, pitch angle, and the number of blades to maximize power output at a specific wind speed? Use the simulation to conduct trials, log data, and find the optimal configuration.
