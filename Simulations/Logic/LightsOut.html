Lights Out

Instructions

  • The goal of the game is to turn all the lights off.
  • Clicking on any light will toggle it, but it will also toggle the lights immediately adjacent to it (above, below, left, and right).
  • Diagonal lights are not affected.
  • Try to solve the puzzle in as few moves as possible.
